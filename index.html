05 — straight answers

The bits everyone asks.

Do I really not need TheMusicLicence?

Not for our music. PRS and PPL collect royalties for artists registered with them. Every track in the Oadio library is sourced from catalogues outside those repertoires, so there's nothing for them to license. We provide a confirmation certificate you can show any inspector.

Can't I just play Spotify or YouTube in my shop?

Consumer accounts are personal-use only — their terms forbid business use, and you'd still owe TheMusicLicence on top. Playing them in a venue risks the 50% licence surcharge and fines up to £5,000 per infringement.

What if my internet drops?

The player buffers hours of music ahead, so a wobbly connection doesn't mean a silent shop. When it's back, everything re-syncs automatically.

What do I play it on?

Anything with a browser — phone, tablet, laptop or till PC into your speakers. No special kit. We can supply a preconfigured plug-and-play box if you'd rather not think about it.

Is the trial really free?

14 days, full library, no card details. Not for you? Walk away — nothing to cancel, no chasing calls.
